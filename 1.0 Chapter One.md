# 🐍 Chapter 1
## The First Steps of the Programmer Monk

There is a Zen proverb that says:

> *"Before enlightenment, chop wood and carry water.*
> *After enlightenment, chop wood and carry water."*

This chapter is **chopping wood and carrying water**.

It is not glamorous.
You will not build artificial intelligences yet.
You will not create revolutionary algorithms.

But without this,
**there is no temple in which to meditate**.

---

## 🌱 Here you will learn:

- 🧘 **Who created Python and why he thought like a monk**
  *(pragmatism over purity, empathy with the reader)*

- 🏯 **Where to write your code**
  *The dojo where you will practice* (development environments)

- 🧭 **How to preserve your work**
  *Git, the eternal memory of code*

- 📜 **The fundamental language**
  Variables, types, operations, and sacred words
